You think your mate is lying?

Rules of


Mr.
Mäxchen


Mr. Mäxchen is a traditional german drinking game known as „Maiern“ / „Mäxchen“ / LYING

  • Minimum of 3 Player around a table – no maximum of people
  • Play with one device, the device will be handed over to the next person
  • Play clockwise
  • In each step you have to choice between saying the truth or lying
  • But your number must be higher than the previous number
  • Order of number:
    31 < 32 < 41 < 42 < 43 < 51 < 52 < 53 < 54 < 61 < 62 < 63 < 64 < 65 < 11 < 22 < 33 < 44 < 55 < 66 < 21

Have fun and take some drinks!
